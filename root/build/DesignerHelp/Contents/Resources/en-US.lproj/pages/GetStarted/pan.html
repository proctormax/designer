Pan/Scroll the Document view

When your document is zoomed-in, you can work on different areas of your design by using panning.

To pan an image with the View Tool:
  1. From the Tools panel on the left, click the View Tool (or shortcut H).
  2. Drag the zoomed-in document in relation to its view.

Temporarily activate the View Tool by using Hot key panning: press . As soon as you let go, the tool will switch back to the previously selected tool.

You can also pan the Document view using your mouse wheel, scroll bars, or the Navigator panel.

For Trackpad/Magic Mouse users, see Gestures.
