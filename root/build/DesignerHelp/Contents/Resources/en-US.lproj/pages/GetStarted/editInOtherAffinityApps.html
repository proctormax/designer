Editing files in other Affinity apps

Affinity apps allow you to edit placed and linked Designer, Photo and Publisher project files in their native apps. This approach helps keep the responsibilities for layout, graphic design and photography separate from each other. Using this approach, respective personnel in a team can develop their own content independently of other disciplines. Linked content will automatically be updated.

To edit content in a different app:
  • With content selected, from the File menu, select Edit in Designer, Edit in Photo or Edit in Publisher.
