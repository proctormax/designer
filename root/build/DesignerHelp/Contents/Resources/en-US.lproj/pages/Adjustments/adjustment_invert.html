Invert adjustment

Create a negative image by inverting all color channels.

This adjustment has no customizable settings.

You can use Invert as part of the process of making edge masks and to apply sharpening and other adjustments to selected areas of an image.

For more control in creating a negative-style (inverted) image, you can use a Levels adjustment.
