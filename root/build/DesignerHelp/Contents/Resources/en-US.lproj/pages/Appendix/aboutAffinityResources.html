About Affinity add-ons

New creative options can be added to your Affinity app by importing Affinity add-ons, including brushes, assets, swatches palettes, object styles, and document templates.

Add-on providers

Affinity add-ons are available from the Affinity Store and third-party providers. Third-party add-ons need to be imported manually.

Add-ons might also be shared with you by team members and clients, e.g. as part of a brand style guide.

Managing Affinity Store add-ons more easily

Add-ons acquired from the Affinity Store can be downloaded from the website and installed manually. However, they can be imported more easily—entirely automatically, even—by using the My Account feature.

Manually importing add-ons

Each type of add-on is imported via the corresponding part of your Affinity app.

  • Assets
  • Brushes
  • Fonts
  • Styles
  • Swatches palettes
  • Document templates
